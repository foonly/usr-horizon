# Federation of Core Systems

## Member Systems

### Vega System

The Vega system is one of the rare systems with two earthlike planets. The Largely Oceanic Estinte (.9G, pop. 600 million) and the smaller but denser Santa Madre (1.2G, pop. 125 million).

It is one of the core systems of the Federation, and the votes of the system is divided between the two planets.

## Federation Warships

### FFG-412 Achilles Class Frigate

A dedicated patrol and escort vessel designed to enforce authority in sectors where larger warships are not required. This ship has an "open" bridge, mounted near the top of the structure.
The shuttle hangar bay can house two shuttle / interceptor ships or four fighters.

#### Armament

- Rail Gun: A twin-barrel railgun (120mm) is mounted along the superstructure.
- Missile Systems: VLS (Vertical Launch System) cells carry 12 heavy anti-ship missiles and tactical nuclear deterrents. 64 Smaller, nimble PD missile launchers provide mid-range defense.
- Close-In Defense: Six CIWS (Close-In Weapon System) autocannon turrets are distributed to cover 360 degrees for point defense.
- E-War: Two dedicated launchers are included for deploying decoys.

### FDG-801 Ajax Class Destroyer

The Ajax is built for heavy fleet engagements and power projection, featuring massive upgrades in firepower and endurance. The Ajax feature an Armoured Command Bridge, and and auxiliary control center.
There is a shuttle bay that can house four shuttles / interceptors, and a fighter bay that houses six fighters.

#### Armament

- Rail Guns: Two heavy, armored twin-barrel Railgun System (160mm), providing devastating kinetic impact at range.
- Missile Systems: VLS (Vertical Launch System) cells carry 48 heavy anti-ship missiles and tactical nuclear deterrents. 96 Smaller, nimble PD missile launchers provide mid-range defense.
- Close-In Defense: Twelve CIWS (Close-In Weapon System) autocannon turrets are distributed to cover 360 degrees for point defense.
- E-War: Two dedicated launchers are included for deploying decoys.

### FCG-327 Eclipse Class Orbital Superiority Cuiser

The Eclipse primary purpose is to control planetary battlefields from orbit. It's not built for fleet battles, and is the biggest of all cruisers. It features two "open" bridges, one for ship operations and one for planetary control.
It has four shuttle hangars, each holding up to four shuttle / interceptor ships, as well as a fighter bay for six fighters.
It also has extra space for troops for launching planetary operations. It's not meant as a troop carrier, but rather mission control for planetary operations.

#### Armament

- Rail Guns: Four quad-barrel Railgun System (120mm), providing orbital bombardment capabilities. And one twin-barrel Railgun System (160mm), providing devastating kinetic impact at range.
- Missile Systems: VLS (Vertical Launch System) cells carry 24 heavy anti-ship missiles and tactical nuclear deterrents. 128 Smaller, nimble PD missile launchers provide mid-range defense.
- Close-In Defense: Twelve CIWS (Close-In Weapon System) autocannon turrets are distributed to cover 360 degrees for point defense.
- E-War: Eight dedicated launchers are included for deploying decoys and drones.
