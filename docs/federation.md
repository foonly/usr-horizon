# Federation of Core Systems

## Member Systems

### Vega System

The Vega system is one of the rare systems with two earthlike planets. The Largely Oceanic Estinte (.9G, pop. 250 million) and the smaller but denser Santa Madre (1.5G, pop. 12 million).

It is one of the core systems of the Federation, and the votes of the system is divided between the two planets.
